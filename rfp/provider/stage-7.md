# 🏢 Stage 7 — Proposal Creation

## 🎯 What the Stage Is For

To compile everything into:

> A structured, compelling response

---

## 👥 Who Is Involved

### Customer Side

| Role | Involvement |
|------|-------------|
| Customer Team | None (internal to vendor) |

---

### Service Provider Side

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Solution Architects | Provide technical content and solution details |
| Sales Team | Align messaging with client expectations |
| Proposal Writers | Structure and articulate the response |
| Marketing (sometimes) | Enhance presentation and branding |

---

## 🛠 What Gets Produced

| Output | Description |
|--------|------------|
| Technical Proposal | Solution design, architecture, and approach |
| Commercial Proposal | Pricing and commercial structure |

### Supporting Documents

| Document |
|----------|
| Case studies |
| Credentials |

---

## ⚠️ What Can Go Wrong

| Risk | Impact |
|------|--------|
| Poor storytelling | Weak client engagement and differentiation |
| Too technical, not business-aligned | Fails to connect with decision-makers |
| Lack of clarity | Confusing or incomplete proposal |
