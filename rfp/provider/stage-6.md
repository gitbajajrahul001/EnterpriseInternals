# 🏢 Stage 6 — Commercial Model & Pricing

## 🎯 What the Stage Is For

To define:

> “How much will this cost and how will we charge?”

---

## 👥 Who Is Involved

### Customer Side

| Role | Involvement |
|------|-------------|
| Procurement | Involved during evaluation phase |
| Finance | Reviews cost and financial viability |

---

### Service Provider Side

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Finance Team | Defines pricing structure and margins |
| Sales | Aligns pricing with deal strategy |
| Delivery Leadership | Validates cost vs delivery feasibility |

---

## 🛠 What Gets Produced

| Output | Description |
|--------|------------|
| Pricing Model | Overall commercial approach |
| Time & Material | Pay for effort |
| Fixed Price | Defined scope with fixed cost |
| Managed Services | Recurring operational cost model |
| Cost Breakdown | Detailed cost components |
| Margin Analysis | Profitability assessment |

---

## ⚠️ What Can Go Wrong

| Risk | Impact |
|------|--------|
| Underpricing | Leads to financial losses during delivery |
| Overpricing | Reduces competitiveness and may lose the deal |
| Hidden costs | Creates trust issues with the client |
