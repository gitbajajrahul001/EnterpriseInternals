## 🎯 Goal

Ensure RFP is:

| Objective |
|----------|
| Complete |
| Risk-free |
| Legally sound |

---

## 👥 Who Is Involved

| Role |
|------|
| CIO / CTO |
| Procurement |
| Legal |
| Risk & Compliance |

---

## 🛠 What Happens

| Function | Activity |
|----------|----------|
| Legal | Reviews liability clauses and penalties |
| Risk Team | Reviews vendor dependency risks |
| IT | Validates technical completeness |

---

## 📦 Output

| Output |
|--------|
| Approved RFP ready for release |

# 🧠 Key Real-World Insights

---

## 1. RFI Is Optional but Very Common in Large Enterprises

Used when:

| Situation |
|-----------|
| Problem is complex |
| Market understanding is needed |

---

## 2. Procurement Drives Process, but IT Defines Content

| Function | Role |
|----------|------|
| Procurement | Process owner |
| Architects | Solution owner |

---

## 3. Most Failures Happen Due To

| Issue |
|-------|
| Poor scope definition |
| Lack of clarity in RFP |
| Unrealistic expectations |
